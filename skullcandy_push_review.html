Skull Candy Push® ANC Actives Review: Premium Sound on a Budget

4.5/5.0


Skull Candy Push® ANC Actives deliver exceptional sound quality and impressive battery life at an affordable price point, making them a standout choice for students and young professionals.

By Jamie Tech April 25, 2025 12 Comments
Earbuds

Finding quality earbuds that don't drain your wallet is becoming increasingly difficult in today's market. Premium features like active noise cancellation, spatial audio, and long battery life usually come with a hefty price tag. That's where the OnePlus Nord Buds 3 come in, promising to deliver high-end audio performance at a student-friendly price point of just $59.99.

I've spent the last two weeks testing these earbuds in various settings – from noisy coffee shops and commutes to quiet study sessions – to see if they truly deliver on this promise. Let's dive into what makes these budget earbuds worth considering for students and young professionals.

Design & Build Quality

The OnePlus Nord Buds 3 feature a sleek, minimalist design reminiscent of their more expensive counterparts. Available in two colors (Midnight Black and Stellar White), these earbuds strike a balance between subtle aesthetics and durability.

The charging case is compact enough to slip into a pocket without creating an unseemly bulge. It features a matte finish that resists fingerprints and scratches – a practical design choice for everyday use. The earbuds themselves are lightweight (4.9g each) and include three sizes of silicone tips to ensure a comfortable fit for most ear shapes.

OnePlus has also included an IPX5 water resistance rating, making these buds suitable for workouts and light rain – a feature often missing from earbuds in this price range.

Sound Quality

For budget earbuds, the sound quality of the Nord Buds 3 is surprisingly impressive. Equipped with 12.4mm titanium drivers, they deliver punchy bass and clear mids without muddying the overall sound profile.

While testing with various music genres, I found that they handle electronic and hip-hop tracks exceptionally well, with bass-heavy songs maintaining clarity even at higher volumes. The high frequencies are adequately crisp though not as detailed as you'd find on premium models costing three times as much.

Active Noise Cancellation

The active noise cancellation (ANC) is where these earbuds truly shine for their price point. While not on par with high-end offerings from Sony or Apple, the Nord Buds 3 can reduce ambient noise by up to 30dB, which is sufficient to dampen the hum of coffee shop conversations or the rumble of public transportation.

There are three noise control modes: Noise Cancellation, Transparency, and Off. The transparency mode allows ambient sound in – handy when you need to stay aware of your surroundings without removing the earbuds.

Battery Life & Charging

Battery performance is another area where the Nord Buds 3 exceed expectations. A single charge provides up to 7 hours of playback with ANC off, and around 5.5 hours with ANC enabled. The charging case adds another 30 hours, bringing the total listening time to an impressive 37 hours.

The quick-charge feature is particularly useful for students rushing between classes – just 10 minutes in the case delivers around 3 hours of playback. The case supports USB-C charging but lacks wireless charging capabilities, which is understandable given the price point.

Connectivity & Features

The Nord Buds 3 utilize Bluetooth 5.3, providing a stable connection with minimal dropouts. They support Google Fast Pair for Android devices, making the initial setup nearly instantaneous. However, iPhone users will need to pair manually through the Bluetooth settings.

OnePlus offers a companion app called HeyMelody that works across both Android and iOS, allowing users to customize touch controls, adjust EQ settings, and update firmware. The app is intuitive and offers more customization than typically found at this price point.

Controls & Smart Features

Touch controls on both earbuds allow you to manage playback, calls, and noise control modes. While there's a slight learning curve to remember which gestures do what, the controls are responsive and rarely trigger accidentally.

The dual microphone array performs admirably for calls, with built-in AI noise reduction that filters out background noise. In my testing, call recipients reported clear voice quality even in moderately noisy environments.

OnePlus has also included compatibility with Google Assistant and Siri, activated with a long press on the right earbud.

Student Life Test: A Week in Review

To properly assess these earbuds for student life, I incorporated them into my daily routine for a full week. Here's how they performed across different scenarios:

Study Sessions

During lengthy study sessions at the library, the comfort factor really shines. I could wear the Nord Buds 3 for 3-4 hours without any discomfort. The ANC effectively blocked out background conversations and typing sounds, creating a focused environment. The battery easily lasted through multiple study sessions without needing to recharge.

Commuting

On bus and subway commutes, the ANC handled engine noise and general commuter sounds admirably. The secure fit meant I never worried about them falling out while moving between classes or running to catch public transportation.

Workouts

The IPX5 rating proved valuable during gym sessions. Even during intense workouts with considerable sweating, they stayed in place and continued functioning perfectly. The bass-forward sound profile provided good motivation during workouts.

Video Lectures

For online lectures and video content, the low latency connection meant there was no noticeable audio delay – crucial for educational content. Voice clarity was excellent, making it easy to follow instructors' explanations.

The Verdict: 8.2/10

The OnePlus Nord Buds 3 represent exceptional value for students and budget-conscious tech enthusiasts. While they don't quite match the audio refinement or feature set of premium options, they nail the essentials with impressive ANC, battery life, and sound quality that punches well above their price class.

At $59.99, they hit the sweet spot between affordability and performance, making them easy to recommend for students looking to maximize their tech budget without major compromises. These earbuds prove that good audio doesn't have to be expensive.
