Xiaomi Smart Band 8 Review: Premium Features on a Student Budget

4.0/5.0


The Xiaomi Smart Band 8 delivers impressive fitness tracking and smart features at an unbeatable price, making it the perfect wearable for budget-conscious students.

By Jamie Tech May 2, 2025 8 Comments
Wearables

In the crowded market of budget fitness trackers, Xiaomi has consistently delivered exceptional value with its Smart Band series. The new Xiaomi Smart Band 8 continues this tradition, packing premium features into a sleek design that costs just $49.99 - less than half the price of most smartwatches.

As a student who's been testing this device for three weeks, I've put it through its paces during lectures, gym sessions, and even sleep tracking to see if it's worth your hard-earned money. Here's everything you need to know about Xiaomi's latest budget fitness tracker.

Design & Display

The Smart Band 8 features a significant design upgrade from its predecessor. The 1.62-inch AMOLED display now has a 60Hz refresh rate, making animations and swipes noticeably smoother. With a resolution of 192 x 490 pixels and 500 nits brightness, everything from workout stats to notifications looks crisp and remains visible even in direct sunlight.

Xiaomi has slimmed down the chassis while making the screen larger, resulting in a more premium look that resembles expensive smartwatches. The silicone strap is comfortable for all-day wear and comes in various colors (I tested the graphite black version). The quick-release mechanism makes swapping bands easy if you want to change up your style.

At just 26g (including strap), you'll barely notice it on your wrist during lectures or workouts. The 5ATM water resistance means you don't need to remove it for showers or swimming sessions - a feature rarely found at this price point.

Fitness & Health Tracking

For students looking to stay active between study sessions, the Band 8 offers comprehensive fitness tracking:

Activity Tracking

The device automatically tracks steps, distance, calories burned, and active minutes throughout the day. During my testing, the step count was within 3% of my manual count over a 1,000-step test - impressive accuracy for a budget tracker.

Workout Modes

With over 150 workout modes (including running, cycling, swimming, and even less common activities like jump rope and HIIT), the Band 8 covers virtually any exercise a student might do. The automatic workout detection worked well for running and walking, starting tracking within 2-3 minutes of activity.

Heart Rate & SpO2 Monitoring

The 24/7 heart rate monitoring provides useful insights into your resting heart rate trends. During workouts, the optical sensor refreshes every second for more accurate readings. SpO2 (blood oxygen) monitoring works well for spot checks, though continuous monitoring isn't available to preserve battery life.

Sleep Tracking

As students, quality sleep is often sacrificed for study sessions. The Band 8's sleep tracking provides detailed breakdowns of light, deep, and REM sleep stages, along with sleep scores and suggestions for improvement. The data correlated well with how rested I felt each morning.

Smart Features

Beyond fitness, the Band 8 offers useful smart features for student life:

Notifications

When paired with your smartphone (both Android and iOS), the Band 8 displays notifications for calls, messages, and apps. You can't reply directly, but being able to quickly check notifications during lectures without pulling out your phone is invaluable.

Music Control

The music controls allow you to play/pause, skip tracks, and adjust volume directly from your wrist - perfect for study sessions with background music.

Find My Phone

A lifesaver for forgetful students, this feature makes your phone ring even when on silent mode.

Weather & Calendar

Quick access to weather forecasts and calendar events helps you plan your day without reaching for your phone.

Battery Life

Battery performance is where budget wearables often cut corners, but the Band 8 shines here. With typical use (continuous heart rate monitoring, sleep tracking, and about 50 notifications daily), I consistently got 12-14 days between charges.

Heavier use with more notifications and frequent GPS-connected workouts still delivered 7-8 days. The magnetic charging cable snaps on easily and fully charges the device in about 1.5 hours.

Student Life Test: Real-World Usage

To evaluate how well the Band 8 fits into student life, I used it exclusively for three weeks across various scenarios:

Lecture Halls

The silent vibrating alarm reliably woke me up without disturbing roommates. Discreet notification previews during classes meant I could stay focused while not missing important messages.

Library Sessions

The "Do Not Disturb" mode silenced notifications during study marathons, while the Pomodoro timer watch face helped maintain productive study intervals.

Campus Life

Automatic step counting accurately tracked my movement between classes, and the lightweight design made it comfortable for all-day wear.

Gym & Sports

The swim tracking worked perfectly for pool sessions, and the various workout modes covered everything from weightlifting to basketball.

The Verdict: 8.5/10

The Xiaomi Smart Band 8 punches far above its weight class, offering premium features at a price that won't break a student's budget. While it lacks some advanced smartwatch capabilities, it excels at the fundamentals of fitness tracking and delivers exceptional battery life.

For students who want reliable activity tracking, sleep monitoring, and basic smart features without spending hundreds on a smartwatch, the Band 8 is easily one of the best values in wearables today. It proves you don't need to sacrifice quality when shopping on a budget.
